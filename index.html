Clients Question

Down bellow we tried to answer commonly asked questions and concerns that have been provided by our customers.

How do I sign up?

Visit our Membership Levels page and purchase a subsciption.

Are application fee waivers available?

When you sign up to the Personal membership you get an amazing lot of deals.


  • Discounted Programs / Camps
  • Events / Open Days
  • Club Newsletter
  • Giveaways
  • Cafe Meal Deals
What does the Family membership give me?

When you sign up to the Family membership your family (2 adults & 2 children) get to share an amazing lot of deals.


  • Discounted Programs / Camps
  • Events / Open Days
  • Club Newsletter
  • Giveaways
  • Cafe Meal Deals
Are application fee waivers available?

Yes but there are some T&C applicable. Please visit this page to know details.

What is included in my membership fee?

Depending on what membership type you select, your membership includes either 3 seasons of practice (Year Membership), 12 practices (Season Membership), or 6 practices of your choosing (Half Season Membership).
